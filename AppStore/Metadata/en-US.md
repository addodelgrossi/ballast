# English (U.S.)

## Name

Ballast

## Subtitle

Grounding on your wrist

## Promotional text

Come back to the present with a private, haptic-first 5-4-3-2-1 grounding exercise for Apple Watch.

## Description

Ballast is a quiet grounding tool for difficult moments.

Start directly from your watch face, close your eyes, and follow the familiar 5-4-3-2-1 sequence through gentle haptic prompts:

• 5 things you see
• 4 things you hear
• 3 things you feel
• 2 things you smell
• 1 thing you taste

Turn the Digital Crown once for each thing you notice. Ballast counts down and advances automatically. The display remains simple and redundant, so the exercise can be completed without relying on reading or precise taps.

Ballast is completely offline. There is no account, advertising, analytics, backend, or exercise history.

Ballast is a grounding tool and is not a medical device or a substitute for professional care.
