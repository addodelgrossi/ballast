Common questions

Does Ballast collect my data?

No. Ballast has no account, backend, analytics, or advertising.

Why can’t I feel haptics in the simulator?

The simulator validates the visual and interaction flow, but haptics must be evaluated on a physical Apple Watch.

Is Ballast medical care?

No. Ballast is a grounding tool, not a medical device, diagnosis, treatment, or substitute for professional care. If you are in immediate danger, contact local emergency services.
