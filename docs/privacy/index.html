Privacy policy

Private by design.

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Data collection

Ballast does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app has no account system, advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, or backend service.

Data stored on your device

The current version does not store exercise history, mood information, health data, or identifiers. Exercise state may remain temporarily in memory while the app is running and is discarded when the process ends.

Permissions and third parties

Ballast does not request HealthKit, location, contacts, microphone, camera, or other sensitive permissions. It does not integrate third-party services.

Children

Because Ballast collects no information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If a future version changes these practices, this policy will be updated before that version is released.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be submitted through the Ballast support tracker.

Política de privacidade

Privado por princípio.

Ballast não coleta, transmite, vende ou compartilha dados pessoais. O app não possui conta, publicidade, analytics, rastreamento, backend ou integrações com terceiros.

A versão atual não armazena histórico de exercícios, humor, dados de saúde ou identificadores e não solicita permissões sensíveis. Dúvidas podem ser enviadas pelo canal de suporte do Ballast.
